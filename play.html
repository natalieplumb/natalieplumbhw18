If I'm Not Designing,
I'm Probably in the Ocean.
Growing up along the California coast, the ocean has always been my home.  It is the source of my creative inspiration, where I am happiest, and where I have met an incredible community of like-minded women.  You may ask how a surfer girl and ocean lifeguard found herself in the world of user experience.  To me, these worlds are one in the same.
In both I fee alive, strong, and above all, at home.

T-Shirt Design
Are you sensing a trend? I love creating functional art.  Here are a few of the t-shirts I have designed for the 2020 UCSB Surf Team and my 2018 Ocean Beach Lifeguard relay team.  If you are familiar with Ocean Beach, San Diego, you might recognize some of the famous surf spots represented by cartoons (“Abs”, “No Surf”, “Osprey”, “Garbage”, “Donuts”, etc.).
